When asked for a status update or progress report, gather:
1. Tasks completed during the current session or task window.
2. Tasks remaining to reach the immediate milestone or project goal.
3. Any active blockers, questions, or decisions required from the human.

Reply in exactly three sections:
**What I Did**
- [Bullet points of completed work]

**What's Left**
- [Bullet points of pending tasks]

**What Needs Your Call**
- [Bullet points of open questions or confirmation requests]
